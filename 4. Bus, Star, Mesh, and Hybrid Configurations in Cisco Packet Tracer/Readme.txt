"Exploring Network Topologies: Bus, Star, Mesh, and Hybrid Configurations in Cisco Packet Tracer"

Embark on a journey through network topologies with this Cisco Packet Tracer project, showcasing four configurations: bus, star, mesh, and hybrid. Each topology presents unique advantages and challenges, offering insights into network design and connectivity strategies. Experience the simplicity of a bus topology, the centralized architecture of a star topology, the robustness of a mesh topology, and the flexibility of a hybrid topology. Whether you're new to networking or an experienced professional, this project provides hands-on experience in creating, configuring, and analyzing diverse network architectures, enriching your understanding of network topology concepts.
