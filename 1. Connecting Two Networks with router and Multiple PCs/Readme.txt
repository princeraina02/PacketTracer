"Simple Network Configuration with Cisco Packet Tracer: Connecting Two Networks with Router and Multiple PCs"

Explore network fundamentals with this Cisco Packet Tracer project, where a single router bridges two distinct networks. With five PCs on one side and a single PC on the other, this simulation showcases subnetting, routing, and network connectivity essentials. Ideal for beginners or professionals, delve into IP addressing, subnet masks, and routing tables for practical insights. Enhance your networking skills with this engaging simulation, offering hands-on experience in network segmentation and connectivity.
